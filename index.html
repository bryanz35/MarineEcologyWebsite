Introduction

The Giant Oceanic Manta Ray (Mobula birostris), also known as the Giant Manta Ray, is the largest species of manta ray. It's name is derived from the Spanish word for blanket or cloak, "Manta", and its also known as the devilfish because of its horn-shaped cephalic fins. Baby rays are called pups, and groups of rays are called squadrons.
